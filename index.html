About Purple Tee LLC

We're building simple, golfer-first iOS apps for effortless tee-time discovery. Our mission is to make finding and booking tee times at public municipal courses as easy as possible.

Local Links puts everything you need in one visual dashboard—availability, pricing, proximity, and value. No complicated logins, no tracking, just straightforward tools to help you get on the course faster.
