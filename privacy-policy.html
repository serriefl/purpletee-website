Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 7, 2026

Purple Tee LLC ("we," "us," or "our") operates the Local Links iOS mobile application. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our app.

1. No User Identity or Personal Data Collection

There is no concept of user identity in Local Links. The app does not require or support accounts, logins, profiles, names, email addresses, phone numbers, or any personally identifiable information (PII). All functionality operates completely anonymously using only your device and publicly available tee-time data.

2. Anonymous Usage and Performance Data

To improve app reliability, performance, and crash reporting, we use Google Firebase (Analytics and Crashlytics). This collects only anonymous, non-identifiable metrics, including:

  • Device type and model (general category, no serial numbers)
  • App version
  • Generalized location derived from IP address (no precise GPS coordinates)
  • Crash logs and performance events

No names, emails, persistent identifiers, or personal details are collected or linked to any individual user.

3. Location Handling

If you enable device location services for proximity-based course ranking, location data is processed locally on your device only and is never sent to our servers or any third party. We use secure, on-device APIs for distance calculations.

4. Data Usage and Sharing

We do not sell, share, rent, or disclose any data for advertising, marketing, or any other commercial purpose beyond the limited processing performed by Google Firebase (governed by Google's privacy terms). Collected data is used solely to fix bugs, improve performance, and enhance app features.

5. Data Security

We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect the limited anonymous data we process. However, no method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure.

6. Children's Privacy

Local Links is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with information, please contact us.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The most current version will always be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or our privacy practices, please contact us at:
